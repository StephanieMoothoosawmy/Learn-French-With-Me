The Present Tense of Regular Verbs
The present tense is one of the first things you will learn in any language. In French, the present tense is used to describe the following:

  • What's happening currently
  • How you are feeling
  • Habits
  • Facts
  • What's going to happen soon

How to conjugate the present tense
In French, there are three types of regular verbs:

  • Verbs ending in -ER (like parler, to talk)
  • Verbs ending in -IR (like choisir, to choose)
  • Verbs ending in -RE (like vendre, to sell)
To conjugate a verb, take the ending (-ER, -IR or -RE) off the infinitive and add the correct ending as shown in the table below.
-ER verbs
parler
-IR verbs
choisir
-RE verbs
vendre
